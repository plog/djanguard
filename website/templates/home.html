What is it ?

Djanguard is designed to monitor the health and performance of web services in a simple and minimalist way.

Our goal was to create something that is straightforward, without any fancy features or unnecessary complexity.

Whether you're managing multiple websites or just want to ensure your services are always available, Djanguard provides the essential tools you need to maintain reliability and efficiency, without any extra fluff.

Djanguard can simulate user interactions and gather detailed request and response data, allowing you to identify issues before they impact your users.
