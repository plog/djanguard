What is it ?

Djanguard is a robust, Django application designed to monitor the health and performance of your web services.

Whether you're managing multiple websites or want to ensure that your services are always available, Djanguard provides the tools you need to maintain reliability and efficiency.

With a simple dashboard, real-time alerts, and in-depth reporting, Djanguard helps you stay in control of your web infrastructure.

Djanguard leverages Django Rest Framework and Celery to provide seamless background monitoring and data collection. Using Playwright,

Djanguard can simulate user interactions and gather detailed request and response data, allowing you to identify issues before they impact your users.
